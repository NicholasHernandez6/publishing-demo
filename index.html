Top Five Largest Protests in U.S. History

The tradition of protests that demand progressive change in the United States continue.

Here are the country's five of the top protests in terms of attendance:

  1. George Floyd Protests nationwide in 2020 with an estimated 26 million protestors.
  2. Earth Day Occuring in 1970, this nationwide protest had an estimated 20 million protestors fighting for environmental issues.
  3. Women's March 2017 The 2017 nationwide protest drew about 5.6 million protestors.
  4. March for Our Lives The student led protest in 2018 against gun violence, drew nearly 2 million people.
  5. Women's March 2018 The nationwide protest drew about 1.5 million people.
